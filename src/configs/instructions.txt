__Instructions:__
View all the achievements you have done and the ones you are yet to complete in the achievements message. Beat the game by completing all 10 achievements.
Use ↖ ⬅ ⬆ ⬇ ➡ ↗ to move around the map.
Use ◀ 🔼🔽▶ to place the selected block in your hotbar.
Use ❌ to close the game view and the channel.
See your remaining health on the healthbar message. Your hearts do not regenerate.
Next up is your hotbar. You have 6 different slots to hold as many items as you can find.
Use :one: :two: :three: :four: :five: :six: to select a slot.
Use :wastebasket: to delete all the items in your selected slot.
